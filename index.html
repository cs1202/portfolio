I Share click on title to view on heroku

I Share is a social sharing application dedicated to all things in and around the home, such as home decor, rennovation and entertaining. Using the tenents of CRUD, I Share allows users to interact by sharing posts, photos and videos with each other, as well as liking and commenting on other users posts. Although I Share can be completely accessed to view and search article posts without any login, the application does use a registration and login feature which allows users to create a personal dashboard to store favorite posts, view and edit their own postings as well as create postings. I Share is completely mobile responsive and uses a MySQL database to store user generated data.

Team Members and Roles Brett Artmann (front end development) Yamini Nigudkar-SCRUM master (backend development) Alina Anam (front end development) Grant Chessman (backend development)
